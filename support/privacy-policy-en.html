Esculappl Privacy Policy

At Esculappl, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information Collection

To provide our services and allow you to register for courses offered by Esculappl, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Training
  • Professional practice
  • Push notification token

Use of Information

The information we collect from you is used to:

  • Allow you to register and participate in courses offered by Esculappl
  • Personalize your experience and respond to your individual needs
  • Improve our application and services
  • Send you important push notifications regarding your courses or our service
  • Contact you regarding your account or our services

Consent

Your consent is required for the processing of your personal data. You can give or withdraw your consent at any time from the "Manage Esculapp Settings" section in the application settings.

Push Notifications

We use push notifications to keep you informed of important updates regarding your courses or our service. You can manage your notification preferences in the application settings.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights or for any questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us via the support section of the application.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and if the changes are significant, we will inform you directly through the application.
