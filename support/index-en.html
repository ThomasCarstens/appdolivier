User Instructions

Sign up for a course

To sign up for a course, log in to your Esculappl account and go to the page of the course you want. Click the "Sign Up" button to begin.

Create a course

If you're an accredited trainer, you can create your own courses on Esculappl. Log in to your account, go to the "My Courses" page, and click "Create a New Course" to get started.

FAQ

What are the prerequisites for signing up for a course?

To sign up for a course, you must have an Esculappl account. Specific prerequisites for each course are listed on the course page.

Can I cancel my course registration?

Yes, you can cancel your course registration up to 7 days before the course starts. Log in to your account and go to the course page to find the cancellation link.
